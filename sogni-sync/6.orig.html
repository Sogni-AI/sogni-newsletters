SOGNI STUDIO TIPS & TRICKS

Change Your Background, Change Your Story 🖼️

A quick guide to transforming your image backgrounds with ControlNet
Looking for a fresh backdrop for your image? It's easier than you think!

1️⃣ Start by using the Segmentation preprocessor in the ControlNet module to isolate your subject.
2️⃣ Hit the Background Mask button to remove the background cleanly.
3️⃣ Select the ControlNet InPaint model, craft a new prompt, and let Sogni generate a brand-new background.

In just a few steps, you’ll have a whole new vibe for your image—whether it’s a cityscape, a dreamy landscape, or something completely unexpected!
