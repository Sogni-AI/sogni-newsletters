New Stars: Kontext, Krea, Chroma v46 + v48 ⭐️

Meet the models for every stage of creation:

  • Kontext lets you start from any image and make precise, plain-English edits while preserving identity and character consistency.
  • Krea [dev] delivers tasteful, commercially-ready photorealism (industry-leading natural skin, natural light, strong text).
  • Chroma v48 [detail] pushes unconstrained creativity with exquisite micro-detail for final assets; and Chroma v46 [flash] is the turbo sibling for rapid exploration, full creative range.
  • In addition, Kontext has Built-in power tools (Auto-Refresh, Kontext-Fill, two-image blend) which let you stack targeted edits fast without rebuilding the whole scene.
An example of Flux Kontext in action in Sogni Web utilizing two reference images.
Flux Krea [dev] leans into an analog-film aesthetic with subtle hue roll-off and believable textures.

Chroma is a compelling model that creates extraordinarily diverse, creative, and uncensored outputs. Chroma v46 [flash] is optimized for 10–12-step renders that make moodboards and storyboard passes near-instant, with a clean handoff to Chroma v48 [detail] for final polish.

Note: FLUX Kontext and Flux Krea only run on the Fast Supernet as premium licensed models which require Premium Spark or SOGNI token to use.

Want even more control? Along with these new models comes new advanced features for power users to eke every angle of new performance out of them: new Schedulers and Time Spacing options.

Premium Spark 💰💎

Premium Sparks are available for purchase through our native and web apps. These generation credits are used for paying for image generation jobs on the Supernet, just like regular Spark Points or SOGNI tokens—except they also give you perks! Buying Premium Spark allows you to:

  • Get boosted to Premium User Tier with access to exclusive features and models
  • Get image generation queue priority and much higher rate limits
  • Get a 7× multiplier for the Artist leaderboard airdrops

As a reminder, Spark Points are non-transferable credits with stable value that can be earned through rewards or purchased in-app, designed for artists who just want to create—no wallets or crypto experience needed.

ℹ️ Available as an in-app purchase on Pocket and Studio, and on Sogni Web. You can top up Premium Sparks with any card via Stripe that supports 135 currencies from 195 countries.
