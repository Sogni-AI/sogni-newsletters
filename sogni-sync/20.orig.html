New iOS App

Sogni Restore

Bring old memories back to life.

Sogni Restore is a new iOS app for AI-powered photo restoration and enhancement, powered by Sogni Intelligence. It brings old, damaged, faded, or poorly scanned photos back to life — preserving the original character of each image without ever making it look over-edited.

  • Fix what time damaged. Scratches, dust, stains, folds, fading, yellowing, and scan imperfections — all addressed automatically.
  • Improve quality, not character. Sharpness and color balance get a boost while the photo still looks like itself.
  • Turn stills into motion. Convert your restored photos into short cinematic videos, or generate new visual variations.
  • Guided by an AI Assistant. The built-in assistant helps you choose the right tools and make edits intuitively.
  • Project-friendly. Edit history, before-and-after comparison, organized projects, and export of images or videos.

Meet the new Creative Assistant

Available now in Sogni Studio and Sogni Pocket.

Three ways to create, depending on how much help you want:

  • Agent mode — chat-first creation. Tell it what you want and it picks models, writes prompts, and runs multi-step workflows for you.
  • Guided mode — chat with controls. The assistant helps, you decide.
  • Manual mode — the classic Sogni workflow, untouched.

It handles image generation, editing, video and music creation, prompt writing, model selection, settings, media analysis, and multi-step flows — and understands the Sogni UI, Supernet, assets, and system logic, while respecting your safety filter settings. You handle the imagination.

Introducing Personas

Save the people in your life. Use them forever.

Personas lets you set an image and profile for yourself, your partner, your kids, your friends, your pets — once they're in, you can mention them by name and they show up. In the photo. In the video. In the right voice.

Define how they should sound in LTX-2.3 audio, add backstory the assistant uses when it builds scripts. It's the difference between generating a person and generating Mark.

Bonus updates: in Sogni Photobooth, you can describe the style you want instead of browsing preset categories. In Sogni 360, the assistant analyzes photos and creates smoother AI-powered transitions.

Photobooth in action — one prompt, eight magazine-cover styles.

Tutorials & techniques

Learn with Sogni

Four techniques worth a closer look this month.

Architecture · Tutorial

From sketch to finished vision

A rough architectural sketch can turn into something real in minutes — materials, colors, structure, landscaping, atmosphere. With Qwen Image Edit and Flux 2 inside Sogni, edits move fast. With Start & End Frame in LTX or WAN, you can turn that first sketch into a transition a buyer instantly connects with.

Tools · Tutorial

PaintOver, when prompts aren't enough

PaintOver lets you guide image generation more directly. Instead of relying only on prompts, paint over specific parts of an image to show exactly what you want to change, add, or refine. The AI regenerates only that area, based on your input. A more precise way to iterate, adjust details, and build the result step by step.

Photobooth · Tutorial

Motion transfer, no prompt required

In Sogni Photobooth, you can create motion transfer without writing a prompt. Use the default settings and get started — no complicated setup, no steep learning curve. Built to keep things simple, so you can focus on creating and becoming whoever you want to be.

SuperApps · Tutorial

Give your product a new dimension

Not every great product speaks for itself visually — and that's normal. Sometimes the difference comes down to format, motion, and atmosphere. In Sogni Super Apps, we turn simple images into polished visuals designed to stand out — including animating your products with gentle motion that makes them pop.
