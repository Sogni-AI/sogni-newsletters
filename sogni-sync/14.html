⚡ SOGNI & Spark

In case you missed it, we’ve already got our users acquainted with Spark, our render credit alternative for non-crypto natives (and those who only care about creating and nothing else).

And now that we have your full attention, let’s quickly remind you Sogni dual-token system:

🔸SOGNI is the on-chain utility token that powers staking, rewards, governance, and deeper ecosystem access.

🔸Spark Points are stable, non-transferable credits you earn or buy in-app, perfect for creators who just want to make art without touching crypto.

The price of the SOGNI token will fluctuate on Mainnet, as it reflects the average market price across exchanges. This means the amount of SOGNI tokens required to pay for the same Supernet job can vary over time for an Artist. Spark Points are introduced to avoid this exposure to price fluctuations. One Spark Point is always pegged to the cost of a base render job, and that never changes.

What happens to Testnet tokens

If you noticed your Spark or SOGNI balances reset, don’t worry. This was announced ahead of the transition and applies to all Testnet tokens, which held no real value and have now been cleared.

From now on, all activity runs on real SOGNI tokens and Spark Points with real value.

Sogni remains free to use, with daily boosts, ongoing rewards, worker mode, and new ways to earn both Spark Points and SOGNI tokens on mainnet.
