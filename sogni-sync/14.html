What happens to Testnet tokens

If you noticed your Spark or SOGNI balances reset, don’t worry. This was announced ahead of the transition and applies to all Testnet tokens, which held no real value and have now been cleared.

From now on, all activity runs on real SOGNI tokens and Spark Points with real value.

Sogni remains free to use, with daily boosts, ongoing rewards, worker mode, and new ways to earn both Spark Points and SOGNI tokens on mainnet.
