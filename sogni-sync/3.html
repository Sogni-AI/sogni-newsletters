SOGNI STUDIO TIPS & TRICKS

Never Lose Your Creative Flow

Three seamless ways to save and restore your settings in Sogni
At Sogni, we make it easy to save and revisit your creative process so you can refine, remix, and evolve your work effortlessly. Here are three ways to save and restore your settings for future sessions:

1. Save & Share as .sogni File

Store your entire project—including prompts, styles, steps, guidance, and even Guide and ControlNet images—as a .sogni file. Open it later to pick up where you left off or share it with other Sogni Studio users.
2. Save to Your Gallery

Love an image? Save it to your gallery, and you can instantly reload all the settings used to create it—or selectively restore only the ones you need.
3. Save Your Custom Styles

Modify built-in presets or craft your own style from scratch, then save it as a custom preset. Your saved styles stay right in the presets list, ready to apply instantly whenever you need them.
With Sogni, your creative process is always in your hands—effortlessly revisit, refine, and evolve your style to create work that’s unmistakably yours.
