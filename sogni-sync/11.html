Keep Creating, Even When Life Interrupts

Sogni Studio & Pocket now reconnects and resumes your creations automatically.
Creating on the go just got smoother. Whether you get a call, check a message, switch apps, or lose connection—Sogni now automatically reconnects and recovers any images in progress. This is especially helpful on iOS, where backgrounding the app used to cut off in-progress generations. Now, you can step away and return without losing a thing. No more starting over. Just create, reconnect, and continue.

Leaderboard & Fast Worker Things

Now Two Weeks into Testnet Phase 4.0, the 30-Day Countdown Begins!

We’re happy to report the Supernet is running the smoothest and fastest it’s ever been during Testnet Phase 4.0 despite ongoing load. Supernet is still seeing 70% of the peak Phase 3.0 load but it's feeling more like 10% load with major enhancements to the model switching and job matching algorithms. This ensures Artists are never left waiting as model demands shift. Countless network upgrades and performance enhancements have been paying off—We're so ready for the next flood of Creatives!

We’ve begun to roll out our next series of worker image updates starting with new User Configured Cooldown settings for Fast Workers. Mac Workers, this update will be coming to you soon.

Our Testnet Staking leaderboard continues to be a huge success. With 138 million testnet tokens staked and burned, that is roughly 138 million renders freed up in the job queue and the Artist leaderboard for new Artists.

If you have been curious about running a Fast Worker on the platform these last 30 tentative days until Mainnet is a great time to experiment with renting a Fast Worker. Please note that Fast Workers are exclusively earning Fast Worker Leaderboard points and rank this testnet phase with a 100% testnet token burn on claim. However with 221 Fast Workers ranked so far this Testnet Phase every worker will be guaranteed an airdrop. Just be sure to review the Terms and Conditions.
