Shape Your Vision: New Aspect Ratios & Sizes

Fine-tune your compositions for any medium, with more flexibility than ever.
Sogni Studio and Sogni Studio Pro now give you the power to craft images in a variety of aspect ratios and sizes—from ultra-wide landscapes to tall portraits and perfectly square compositions. With over 10 presets, you can compose your images exactly to fit your desired output ratio and final medium, whether for digital art, prints, or social media.

Finding the right frame for your vision has never been easier. Whether you’re designing cinematic scenes, mobile-friendly artwork, or anything in between, these new options help you shape your creations exactly how you imagine them.

Plus, Standard models now support up to 1024px resolution, a big jump from the previous 786px limit—giving you more detail and flexibility than ever before.

Shape your vision, fine-tune your compositions, and create with precision! Try it out today.
