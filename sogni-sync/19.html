Your New Video Superpowers 🎬

Sogni Studio is upgraded with new ways to bring your images to life.

Motion Transfer: Animate-Move & Animate-Replace

Use any video to drive motion in your creations. Two modes:

Animate-Move: Apply motion from a source video to your character. Your character keeps its look while inheriting the movement.

Animate-Replace: Keep the original video's motion and scene, swap out the subject for your character.

The wild part? You can record directly from your Mac's webcam. Capture your own movements and transfer them to any AI character in seconds.
👉 Motion Transfer Docs

Sound to Video

Drop an audio track. Watch your image dance to it.

Sound to Video analyzes your audio's rhythm and dynamics, then generates motion that matches. Works beautifully for music-driven visuals, ambient scenes, and lip-sync.

Pro tip: Use the mic button to record live audio directly in the app.
👉 Sound to Video Docs

Change Perspective

Re-render your image from any camera angle while keeping the subject and style consistent.

Choose your distance (closer or wider), orientation (front, left, right, back, diagonals), and elevation (low to high). Same subject, new viewpoint.

Available in Sogni Studio Pro and now in Sogni Pocket too.
👉 Change Perspective Docs
 

Clip Mixer Gets Pro Features ✂️

The Clip Mixer now handles professional video editing workflows:

• Trim/Keep Last Frame -- Prevent repeated frames at clip boundaries for seamless transitions.
• Mute/Unmute Clips -- Control audio on individual clips.
• Additional Audio Track -- Add music or voiceover that plays across all clips.

Three Export Modes:

• Fast Combine (no re-encode, instant)

• High Quality (one-pass, recommended)

• Share-safe (full re-encode for maximum compatibility)

Multi-Angle Magic in Photobooth 📸

Mark's been cooking. The new 3D Camera Angle feature in Photobooth gives you 96 different perspectives from a single image -- adjusting distance, height, and rotation.

But here's where it gets good: uncheck the single-image box to unlock multi-angle presets and video workflows.

The workflow:

1. Generate your image with angle controls
2. Pick a multi-angle preset or build your own
3. Regenerate each angle until it's perfect
4. Save as a Local Project for reuse
5. Run Batch Transition to stitch into video
6. Get a seamlessly looping orbit video
👉 Try Photobooth
 

Sogni 360: Orbit Videos from a Single Shot 🔄

Sogni 360 takes a single image and generates an orbit video. No tripod. No gimbal. No asking a stranger to walk around you while filming.

Upload a photo or take a selfie and the AI handles the camera movement. You get a smooth, looping video that shows you (or your subject) from every angle.

Works for travel shots, product showcases, or any moment that deserves more than one perspective.

Mark shipped a new mini-app. Stand still, get a video that moves around you.
👉 Try it at 360.sogni.ai

Now Live: LTX-2 Cinematic Video 🎬

LTX-2 is live across the Sogni ecosystem: in the SDK, in Sogni Create via our Chrome web app, and in Sogni Photobooth.

4K Resolution -- Up to 2160p, 9x the pixel density of standard models.

Unified Audio -- Generate synchronized dialogue, sound effects, and music in a single pass.

5x Faster -- Approximately 5x faster and 5x more cost-effective per frame than Wan 2.2.

IC-LoRA Controls -- Depth, Canny, and Pose controls for precise character motion and architectural structure.

For Developers: LTX-2 is available via the Sogni Client SDK for building with 4K video infrastructure.
